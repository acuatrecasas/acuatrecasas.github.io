I'm relentlessly curious — from first principles to the questions that keep me up at night. Obsessed with spending time on what matters. I chase happiness, and try to bring it to others.

I often think about the infinity of the universe and the tininess of our presence in it. One person, one planet, one universe — too small to fail.

I believe in clear goals. In simplicity as a form of excellence. And in being a good person as a non-negotiable.

Raised in Barcelona. Living in North Carolina.

This is my space to write about what I think, what I learn, what I question, and what matters to me.
